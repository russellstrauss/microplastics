The Danger of Single-use Plastics

With 7.7 billion people on planet Earth as of 2019, the use of single-use plastics presents a dangerous habit that society has adopted. With populations rising at exponential rates and billions of single-use plastics being used and discarded every single day, the rate at which we use plastic is far out of proportion with the time it takes for that plastic to break down. While we continue to pile on the plastic each day, there doesn't yet seem to be any catalyst to develop a timely solution. See how the plastic you use on a daily basis, possibly even a coffee cup or plastic bottle you used today will be around long after you, your children, and grandchildren are no longer living.
